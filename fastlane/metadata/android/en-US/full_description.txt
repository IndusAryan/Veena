Veena (वीणा) is an addon-based music discovery, downloader, and streaming aggregator.

Features:
- Addon Architecture: Modular system for discovery, streaming, and downloading.
- Multi-API Support: Search across multiple sources simultaneously using custom addons.
- Music Downloader: High-quality music downloads with metadata tagging.
- Powerful Streamer: Seamless streaming experience with ExoPlayer/Media3 integration.
- Material 3 & Haze: Modern UI with glassmorphic blur effects and dynamic colors.
- Privacy-Focused: No built-in providers; users control their sources.
- Local Library: Manage your downloaded music with a built-in library manager.

Veena does not collect any user data or telemetry. As a content aggregator, it relies on user-provided addons. All data exchange happens directly between your device and the service provider defined in the addon.
